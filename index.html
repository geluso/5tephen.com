professional work by Steve Geluso

Steve Geluso

Steve Geluso collaborated with Robyn Miller, co-creator of the best-selling video game Myst, to build two websites promoting the film The Immortal Augustus Gladstone. theimmortalaugustusgladstone.com shows off trailers, stills, and songs from the soundtrack to the movie. awol-in-ptown.com is an interactive gigapixel photograph of downtown Portland, taken by Bruce Ely. The image includes hidden characters and items from the movie. Players compete for high scores searching for the hidden images using SONARVISION.

Managing the 67,325 tiles comprising the gigapixel image was a unique challenge. Changes made at one zoom level had to propogate to all other zoom levels. Photoshop automation helped us handle this with ease.

The projects were a great chance to work with Robyn Miller, a creative genius, and David Corry and Alex Miller, who are both gifted programmers.

Additionally, Steve spends his time on personal projects.

Thirtybuckrainiertee.com sells hand-stitched shirts with the famous Ranier logo on them. The site uses Stripe to process payments and accept orders.

This single-player playable version of the Settlers of Catan is an ongoing effort to mimic and toy with the mechanics of the popular board game. The replica is implemented without using any third party graphics or game libraries. It's all created with just JS and HTML5 canvas.

Steve is currently seeking employment.
